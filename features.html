Features of Pradhaan

  • Home
  • Features

Welcome to the Pradhaan Features page! Discover the unique tools and functionalities that make the Pradhaan app the ultimate platform for local leadership, community engagement, and addressing local issues. Whether you're here to share your stories, participate in polls or become a leader our features are designed to help you make a difference.

1. Community Leadership Platform

Become a Leader:

  • Share Your Voice: Share your stories, ideas, and concerns to engage with your community.
  • Rise Through the Ranks: Gain votes from your peers to move from ward to district, state, and even national leadership positions.
  • Weekly Elections: Participate in elections held every Sunday from 9 PM to 10 PM to become a Pradhaan and lead your area.

Your Path to Leadership:

  • Ward > District > State > National
  • Earn Votes: Share impactful posts and gather votes from your community.
  • Weekly Recognition: Top users are recognized as leaders based on community votes.

2. Story Sharing and Promotion

Share Your Stories

  • Local Updates: Post stories about your ward, district, state, or national issues.
  • Visibility: Your stories can be promoted to higher levels based on user votes.

Voting System:

  • Engage and Vote: Vote on stories and posts shared by others.
  • Top Stories: The most popular posts receive greater visibility and reach.

3. Pradhaan’s Board

Pradhaan’s Updates and Important Announcements

  • Key Issues and Updates: Use Pradhaan’s Board to highlight major issues, share important announcements, or address pressing matters.
  • Inform and Engage: Communicate crucial updates and insights to users in your area, fostering awareness and discussion.

Key Features:

  • Important Announcements: Post significant updates and issues that are on Pradhaan’s mind.
  • Community Awareness: Keep your area informed about key events, initiatives, or matters requiring attention.

4. Pradhaan Poll

Participate in Community Polls

  • Voice Your Opinion: Engage in polls covering various topics within your ward, district, state, and nation.
  • See the Results: Share your views and see what others in your area think.

Poll Features:

  • Create Polls: Initiate and participate in polls on diverse issues.
  • Influence Discussions: Contribute to the collective voice of your community.

5. Pradhaan’s Top Pick

Highlight Important Stories

  • Top Story Selection: As Pradhaan, choose the most significant story in your area.
  • Drive Community Action: Bring attention to critical local issues and initiatives.

Key Features:

  • Showcase Top Stories: Select and feature stories that matter.
  • Influence Community Focus: Guide discussions on important local matters.

6. Pradhaan Special Comment

Engage with Every Post

  • Comment on Posts: As a Pradhaan, provide feedback on every post in your area.
  • Foster Community Dialogue: Offer appreciation, advice, and constructive criticism.

Comment Features:

  • Direct Engagement: Interact with community posts through comments.
  • Guide Discussions: Help steer conversations and offer leadership insights.

7. Pradhaan Power

Upgrade Stories Across Levels

  • Promote Significant Stories: Use your Pradhaan Power to upgrade one story from ward level to district, from district to state, and from state to national level.
  • Maximize Impact: Elevate important stories to a broader audience, ensuring they receive the attention they deserve.

Key Features:

  • Story Upgrades: Upgrade stories to higher levels for greater visibility.
  • Broaden Reach: Amplify significant issues or achievements to the next level of the community.

8. Local News Updates

Stay Informed

  • Real-Time News: Receive updates from your ward, district, state, and national levels.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Stay up-to-date with the latest local events and issues.

News Features:

  • News Feed: Access a feed of the latest local news and updates.
  • Engage with News: Comment on articles, share opinions, and discuss issues with community members.

9. Sachiv’s Role

Upgrade Posts

  • Empower Important Stories: As Sachiv, you can upgrade posts to give them more visibility.
  • Support Valuable Content: Ensure that significant issues and stories receive the attention they deserve.

Sachiv Features:

  • Upgrade Content: Boost the visibility of important posts.
  • Influence Community Awareness: Play a key role in highlighting relevant issues.

10. Promotional and Motivational Content

Motivational Videos

  • Inspire and Educate: Watch videos that encourage personal growth and community engagement.
  • Learn from Leaders: Gain insights from successful community leaders and influencers.

Content Features:

  • Video Library: Access a collection of motivational and educational videos.
  • Stay Motivated: Get inspired to work for personal and community improvement.

11. Pledge to Vote

National Election Pledge

  • Commit to Voting: Take a pledge to vote in the upcoming national election.
  • Support Important Issues: Voice your support for issues or candidates that matter to you.

Pledge Features:

  • Make a Commitment: Show your dedication to participating in the democratic process.
  • Encourage Others: Inspire others to take part in the national election.

Get Started with Pradhaan Today!

Explore these features and more to become a leader in your local community. Join the Pradhaan revolution, address local issues and make your voice heard in local politics! Download the Pradhaan app today.
